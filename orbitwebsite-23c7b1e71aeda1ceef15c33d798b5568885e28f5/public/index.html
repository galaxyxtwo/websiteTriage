About

Filecoin Orbit

We are a group of Filecoin and IPFS enthusiasts & located in Austin, TX.

The goal of our community is to bring people together to talk about the future of the Internet. Filecoin and IPFS are helping to build a more resilient, and open internet... that's an exciting future!

Come learn about the industry, how to build on the Open Web, and have some free food.

Come learn at Capital Factory in downtown Austin!
